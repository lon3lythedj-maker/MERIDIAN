MEYERSDAL · AREA GUIDE
MEYERSDAL
// Alberton · Ekurhuleni · 1447

LIFE IN
MEYERSDAL.

A well-established residential suburb in the south of Johannesburg — known for its large stands, mature trees, and family-oriented community. All amenities listed are verified within the Meyersdal boundary.

// Education
SCHOOLS IN THE AREA
Meyersdal Primary School
Public · Grade R–7
One of the area's most established primary schools, known for strong academics and active parent involvement. Walking distance from most Meyersdal properties.
⊕ Open in Maps
Trinityhouse Meyersdal
Private · Grade R–12
A highly regarded independent school offering a Cambridge-aligned curriculum from Grade R through Matric. Strong arts, sport, and STEM programmes.
⊕ Open in Maps
Alberton High School
Public · Grade 8–12
A long-standing secondary school in Alberton with a reputation for sport and extracurriculars. Matric pass rates consistently above the provincial average.

Laerskool Alberton
Public · Afrikaans Medium · Grade R–7
A highly regarded Afrikaans primary school with excellent academic results and a strong cultural programme. Popular with families across Alberton and Meyersdal.

Hoërskool Alberton
Public · Afrikaans Medium · Grade 8–12
One of the largest Afrikaans high schools in Ekurhuleni. Exceptional sporting facilities and a dedicated academic staff with strong matric outcomes.

// Lifestyle
ACTIVITIES & AMENITIES
Meyersdal Nature Reserve
Nature · Outdoor · Family
A 500-hectare urban nature reserve on the doorstep of the suburb. Home to zebra, wildebeest, and over 150 bird species. Popular for hiking, trail running, and weekend walks.
⊕ Open in Maps
Meyersdal Eco Estate
Residential Estate · Security · Recreation
A sought-after eco-focused residential estate adjacent to the nature reserve. Features 24-hour security, communal green spaces, and walking trails throughout.
